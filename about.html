About us

We are a group of people from all around the world, who strive to speak confidently in any scenario.

We meet every Sunday at -4 GMT on zoom and give speeches, presentations, debate each other and much more!
Our meetings are in English and all levels of English are welcome to come and sharpen their language skills!

We R Ok Gavel Club is affiliated with Toastmasters International™.

Ms. Cave

Daria Cave is the Coordinator of We R Ok International Gavel Club. She is a Division champion Toastmaster earning ten triple crowns before starting We R Ok club. She started her professional life as a Broadcaster as a teenager. Then she was asked to be an InternationalAdvocate for women and childrens' rights. She lives in Barbados where she writes and teaches.
